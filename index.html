About CuRec

An attempt to mitigate the COVID-19 pandemic in Sri Lanka, CuRec is a mobile and web application developed by the Faculty of Computing to link the doctors and patients during the COVID-19 Pandemic. CuRec stands for Cure and Recover. Hence, it addresses the problem of identifying potential COVID-19 patients. CuRec offers features such as self-assessment, real-time video calling facility with a qualified health official and location tracking.

Our Mission

To provide a convenient method for citizens to self-assess themselves and facilitate with a medium to

communicate with a qualified health official.

Our Vision

Reduce the number of Deaths in sri lanka.
